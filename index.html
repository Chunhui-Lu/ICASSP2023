Audio samples from "EFFECTIVE ONE-SHOT VOICE CLONING WITH MULTIFACETED SPEAKER REPRESENTATION"

Authors: Chunhui Lu, Xue Wen, Liming Song

Abstract:
Voice cloning is particularly challenging in the one-shot scenario, mainly because of increased uncertainty of speaker characteristics that necessarily accompanies limited observation. In this work we aim at improving speaker similarity in the one-shot setup by explicitly conditioning on several perceptually salient dimensions of speaker variance. In particular, we identify features responsible for timbral, phonetic and prosodic variances of speech, and train the synthesizer to transfer such features from enrolment example to synthetic speech, so that the latter imitates the former in multiple meaningful ways simultaneously. We compare alternative designs for defining and applying these features, and study the effectiveness of our scheme under different enrolment setups.
 

This pages contains a set of speech samples used in our subjective evaluations.All utterances and speakers are unseen in model training.
